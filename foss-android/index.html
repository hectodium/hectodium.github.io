FOSS Android catalog

This is my personal catalog of Free and Open Source Software Android apps that I use on my own device in order to substitute proprietary ones as much as possible. I try to aggregate all the relevant options in each category, compare them and give my opinion on my favourite ones. I tend to prioritize protective licenses (GPL), design and user experience. My vision is to make a suite of apps that can be recommended to the average Android user for easy transition into a FOSS ecosistem, given how fragmented and confusing it can be to a non-technical user.

I will provide F-Droid and Obtainium links to download the apps when possible. I personally use Obtainium to download and update my apps. Down you can find an Obtainium config export with my ideal set of apps for your own use.
