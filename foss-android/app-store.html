App Store

The main app to replace is the Google Play Store. Which is not a small feat, given how integrated and essential are the Google Play Services in the modern Android experience. Other examples of proprietary app stores to be replaced would be the Samsung Galaxy Store, the Huawei AppGallery or the Amazon Appstore.

The most notable FOSS Android app stores are:
